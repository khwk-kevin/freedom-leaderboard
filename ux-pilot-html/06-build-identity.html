Build Strategy & Notes

Playstyle

This build revolves around stacking Critical Strike Chance to 100% and then dumping all remaining stats into Critical Damage. Use Phantom Dash to engage, immediately followed by Infernal Strike for massive burst damage.

Keep Nature's Guard active at all times to prevent one-shots from boss mechanics. Your lifesteal will keep you topped up as long as you are dealing damage.

Weaknesses

  • Extremely vulnerable to Stun and Freeze effects.
  • Requires constant positioning; standing still means death.
  • Mana hungry without the Mana Leech support gem.
